I am a Newcastle based Graphic Communications Advisor and Student RIBA Part I Architect, Stage III. Originally from Barton-Upon-Humber, I studied A-Level Graphic Communication and Design, alongside Criminology, and Mathematics, at Wyke Sixth Form College. I currently study at Newcastle University after receiving offers of study from Bath, MSA, Birmingham City and Northumbria.

I've received the Newcastle University+Napper Memorial Prize for exceptional work in my Stage II Study, alongside working with Medical Architecture as part of the RIBA's Future Architects Mentor Programme. 

With deep interests in politics, sociology and crime, I pursue architecture understanding the human interactions, with a deep interest in Forensic Architecture. In my studio projects I aim to create cutting edge sustainable designs, whilst exploring contemporary materiality. Technology plays a huge role in my design process and outputs, with a complex understanding of Sustainable Building Systems management and technologies, CAD, and Render Softwares.
